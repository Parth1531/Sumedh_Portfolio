About Me

I'm a visual artist focused on stylized character design and visual storytelling. My work spans character concepts, penciling, environment design, and digital illustration, with an emphasis on strong drawing fundamentals, expressive design, and thoughtful composition.

I'm passionate about developing my skills across comics, storyboarding, and pre-production, always striving to create engaging visuals that communicate ideas clearly and leave a lasting impression.
